Oxford Mathematics
Team Challenge

The OMTC is a maths competition for students in Year 11, 12, or 13. We're trying to make a competition that makes fascinating maths feel approachable, even for students without experience with supercurricular maths or olympiad practice.

The OMTC involves:

10:00am - 10:30am

Individual Round

The first round is a short warm-up round that students do individually.
It has 10 questions, but there's no points at stake.

Read more

11:05am - 11:50am

Lock-in Round

Two questions, each split into a number of parts. Each one is designed to deeply explore a new mathematical concept.

Intriguing!

1:30pm - 2:30pm

Maps Round

A fun round where teams explore a 9x9 grid of questions. Connect a path of right answers to the center and score double!

Sounds fun!

2:50pm - 4:10pm

Guts Round

The final and hardest challenge of the day - complete 9 increasingly difficult sets of problems, with a live leaderboard.
