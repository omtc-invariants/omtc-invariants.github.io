Oxford Mathematics
Team Challenge

The OMTC is a maths competition for students in Year 11, 12, or 13. We're trying to make a competition that makes fascinating maths feel approachable, even for students without experience with supercurricular maths or olympiad practice.

You can't register for the OMTC just yet. If you're extra eager to sign up, then join our mailing list and get notified as soon as it starts!
